Weapon Skills
-------------

All have a basic single target attack for start.


2nd skills

Slashing (High damage output single target)
--------
Cleave (hits infront of player multiple targets) -> Spin (spin 360 and strike all enemeys in the area)


Blunt (Tank based, low damage, threat gen)
-----
knockback (Hits enemey backwards away form player) -> Ground Slam (Smaller knockback but full circle multi target)


Peirce (medium damage, longer ranged, chance for multi hit)
------
Charge (rush forward higher damage and chance to hit second target) -> Stabby Stabby (hits multiple times in a line, higher chance to hit multi targets)


Range
-----
Multi shot (cone damage of arrow spray) -> Barage (area of damage)
