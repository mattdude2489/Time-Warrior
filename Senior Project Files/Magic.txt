Damage Types
------------
Fire - Ice/water - Lightning/Energy- Nature/Holy(some sort of healing) - Raw Magic

Raw Magic
---------
Magic Missle -> Missle Barage -> Magic Detonate


Fire(Dot effect)
----
Fire ball(single target hit) -> Fire Blast(explodes after hit) -> Rain of Fire(aoe)


Ice/Water(slows)
---------
Ice bolt(single target slow) -> Ice mist(aoe slow from character) -> Blizzard(aoe)


Lightning/Energy(stun/crit/magnitism/burn)
----------------
Lightning bolt(single target) -> Energy wave(aoe form character) -> Chain Lightning(target to target)


Nature/Healing
--------------
Heal(Single target heal) -> Heal Barier(heals and sets sheild) -> Heal All(aoe heal)
